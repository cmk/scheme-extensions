Description

Recursion schemes and streaming metamorphisms for Mu.

Extends data-fix with paramorphisms, zygomorphisms, Elgot algebras, mutual recursion, pre/postpromorphisms, and generic streaming metamorphisms (Gibbons).

No Recursive/Corecursive typeclasses — all functions operate directly on Mu.
